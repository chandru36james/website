Expanding Global Horizons with Arctic Textiles

Weaving Excellence, Delivering Quality Worldwide

In today’s design-driven world, where comfort and sustainability matter more than ever, Arctic Textiles stands at the forefront of home textile manufacturing and export. From our roots in Karur, Tamil Nadu, India, we have grown into a globally trusted supplier, serving homes, hotels, and retail chains across continents.

Combining traditional textile craftsmanship with advanced production techniques, we deliver high-quality products that enrich interiors with elegance, function, and enduring value. From essentials like bed sheets and towels to statement pieces like custom cushions, throws, and curtains, we offer a wide spectrum of textile solutions designed to enhance everyday living.

Our Vision and Commitment

Established with a vision to elevate India’s home textile presence on the global stage, Arctic Textiles is committed to excellence, ethical manufacturing, and innovative design. Our goal is to deliver products that meet international standards in style, sustainability, and reliability.
